Documentos com knitrBootstrap

Usando knitrBootstrap

Instalar knitrBootstrap e carregar os pacotes

R source 
library(knitr)
library(knitrBootstrap)
library(rmarkdown)

Colocar como YAML fron-matter

---
output:
  knitrBootstrap::bootstrap_document:
    title: "Alguma coisa
    theme: Spacelab
    highlight: Zenburn
    theme.chooser: TRUE
    highlight.chooser: TRUE
---

Escreva o arquivo Rmd e renderize com

R source 
render("arquivo.Rmd", "knitrBootstrap::bootstrap_document")

E veja a mágica acontecer!
